Name: Kettlebell One Arm Snatch
Description: This exercise involves using a kettlebell to perform a one arm snatch movement, which involves lifting the kettlebell from the ground to overhead in one fluid motion. It is a full body exercise that targets the shoulders, back, legs, and core.
Muscle Group: Calves,Front Deltoid,Glutes,Hamstrings,Quadriceps,Shoulders,Side Deltoid,Triceps
