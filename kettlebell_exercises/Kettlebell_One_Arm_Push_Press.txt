Name: Kettlebell One Arm Push Press
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell in one hand and performing a push press movement, which involves bending the knees and using the legs to help drive the kettlebell overhead. It primarily targets the shoulders, triceps, and core muscles.
Muscle Group: Front Deltoid,Shoulders
