Name: Kettlebell One Arm Clean and Jerk
Description: This exercise involves lifting a kettlebell with one arm from the ground to the shoulder, then pushing it overhead in a jerking motion. It is a full-body exercise that targets the shoulders, back, legs, and core.
Muscle Group: Biceps,Calves,Forearms,Front Deltoid,Glutes,Hamstrings,Quadriceps,Shoulders,Side Deltoid,Triceps
