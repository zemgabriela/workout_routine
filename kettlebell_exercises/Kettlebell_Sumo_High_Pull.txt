Name: Kettlebell Sumo High Pull
Description: This exercise involves using a kettlebell and performing a sumo stance while pulling the kettlebell up towards the chest. It targets the muscles in the legs, hips, and upper back.
Muscle Group: Biceps,Calves,Forearms,Front Deltoid,Glutes,Hamstrings,Hips,Quadriceps,Side Deltoid
