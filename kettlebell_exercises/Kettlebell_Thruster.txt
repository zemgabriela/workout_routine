Name: Kettlebell Thruster
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell at shoulder height, squatting down, and then using the momentum from standing up to press the kettlebell overhead. It is a full-body exercise that targets the legs, core, and shoulders.
Muscle Group: Calves,Front Deltoid,Hamstrings,Quadriceps,Shoulders,Side Deltoid,Triceps
