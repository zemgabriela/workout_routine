Name: Kettlebell Straight Leg Deadlift
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell with both hands and bending forward at the hips while keeping the legs straight. The movement targets the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back muscles.
Muscle Group: Glutes,Hips,Spine
