Name: Kettlebell Box Squat
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell in front of your chest while performing a squat with your feet on a box or bench. It targets the lower body muscles, including the glutes, quads, and hamstrings, while also engaging the core and upper body.
Muscle Group: Glutes,Hips,Quadriceps
