Name: Kettlebell One Arm Row
Description: This exercise involves using a kettlebell to perform a rowing motion with one arm, targeting the back muscles. The movement involves pulling the kettlebell towards the body while keeping the core engaged and maintaining proper form.
Muscle Group: Abs,Back,Latissimus Dorsi,Traps
