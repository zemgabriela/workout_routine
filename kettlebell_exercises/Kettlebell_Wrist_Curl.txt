Name: Kettlebell Wrist Curl
Description: The kettlebell wrist curl is an exercise that targets the forearm muscles. It involves holding a kettlebell with an overhand grip and curling the wrist upwards, then lowering it back down. This exercise can help improve grip strength and wrist mobility.
Muscle Group: Forearms
