Name: Kettlebell Split Snatch
Description: This exercise involves using a kettlebell to perform a split snatch movement, which involves lifting the kettlebell from the ground to overhead while simultaneously splitting the legs into a lunge position. It is a full-body exercise that targets the legs, core, and shoulders.
Muscle Group: Hips
