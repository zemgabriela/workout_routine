Name: Kettlebell Lateral Lunge
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell in one hand and stepping to the side with the opposite leg, bending the knee and lowering the body into a lunge position. The movement targets the glutes, quads, and inner thighs while also engaging the core and improving balance.
Muscle Group: Thighs
