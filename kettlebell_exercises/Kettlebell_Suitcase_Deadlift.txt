Name: Kettlebell Suitcase Deadlift
Description: This exercise involves lifting a kettlebell with one hand while keeping the other hand free and extended to the side, mimicking the action of carrying a suitcase. It primarily targets the muscles in the legs, back, and core.
Muscle Group: Hips
