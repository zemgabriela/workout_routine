Name: Kettlebell Alternating Hang Clean
Description: This exercise involves using a kettlebell to perform alternating hang cleans, which involves lifting the kettlebell from a hanging position and bringing it up to the shoulder in a fluid motion. This exercise targets the shoulders, back, and core muscles.
Muscle Group: Biceps,Forearms,Upper Arms
