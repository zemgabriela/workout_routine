Name: Kipping Muscle Up
Description: This exercise involves using a swinging motion to generate momentum and pull oneself up and over a set of rings or bar, transitioning from a hanging position to a support position. It requires a combination of upper body strength, coordination, and timing.
Muscle Group: Abs,Latissimus Dorsi,Waist
