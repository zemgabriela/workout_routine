Name: Kettlebell Deck Squat
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell at chest level while performing a squat and then returning to a standing position. It targets the lower body muscles, including the glutes, quads, and hamstrings, while also engaging the core and upper body.
Muscle Group: Back,Hips,Thighs,Waist
