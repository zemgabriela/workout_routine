Name: Kettlebell Half Turkish Get-up
Description: This exercise involves using a kettlebell to perform a half Turkish get-up, which involves lying on your back, holding the kettlebell with one hand, and using your core and arm strength to lift yourself up to a seated position. The exercise is great for building core and upper body strength, as well as improving balance and stability.
Muscle Group: Thighs
