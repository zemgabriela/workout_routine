Name: Kettlebell Deadlift
Description: This exercise involves lifting a kettlebell from the ground using a deadlift motion, which primarily targets the muscles in the legs and lower back. It is a great exercise for building overall strength and improving posture.
Muscle Group: Glutes,Hips,Quadriceps,Thighs
