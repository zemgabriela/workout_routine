Name: Kettlebell Windmill
Description: The Kettlebell Windmill is a strength training exercise that targets the core, shoulders, and hips. It involves holding a kettlebell overhead with one arm while bending and rotating the torso to touch the opposite foot. The exercise requires stability and mobility in the shoulders and hips, making it a challenging movement for many people.
Muscle Group: Obliques,Waist
