Name: Kettlebell Russian Twist
Description: This exercise involves sitting on the floor with your legs bent and feet flat on the ground, holding a kettlebell with both hands. You then twist your torso to one side, bringing the kettlebell to touch the ground beside you, before twisting to the other side and repeating the movement. This exercise targets the obliques and core muscles.
Muscle Group: Waist
