Name: Kettlebell Sumo Squat
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell with both hands and standing with feet wider than shoulder-width apart in a sumo stance. From there, the individual performs a squat, keeping their back straight and pushing their hips back. The exercise targets the legs, glutes, and core muscles.
Muscle Group: Hips
