Name: Kettlebell Single Arm Thruster
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell in one hand and performing a squat, followed by pressing the kettlebell overhead in one fluid motion. It is a full-body exercise that targets the legs, core, shoulders, and arms.
Muscle Group: Shoulders,Thighs
