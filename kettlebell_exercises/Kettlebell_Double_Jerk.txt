Name: Kettlebell Double Jerk
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell in each hand and performing a double jerk movement, which involves bending the knees and explosively pushing the kettlebells overhead while simultaneously dropping into a partial squat. The movement is then reversed to return to the starting position. This exercise targets the shoulders, triceps, and legs.
Muscle Group: Shoulders
