Name: Kettlebell Swing
Description: This exercise involves swinging a kettlebell between the legs and up to shoulder height, using the momentum generated by the hips and legs. It primarily targets the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back, while also engaging the core and shoulders.
Muscle Group: Front Deltoid,Glutes,Hamstrings,Hips
