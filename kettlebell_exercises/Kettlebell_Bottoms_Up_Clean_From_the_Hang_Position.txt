Name: Kettlebell Bottoms Up Clean From the Hang Position
Description: This exercise involves using a kettlebell and starting in a hang position. The kettlebell is then cleaned up to a bottoms up position, where the handle is pointing towards the ceiling. This exercise helps to improve grip strength, shoulder stability, and overall body control.
Muscle Group: Forearms,Upper Arms
