Name: Kettlebell Pistol Squat
Description: This exercise involves holding a kettlebell in one hand while performing a pistol squat, which is a one-legged squat where the non-working leg is extended in front of the body. It targets the lower body, particularly the quads, glutes, and hamstrings, as well as the core for stability.
Muscle Group: Glutes,Quadriceps,Thighs
